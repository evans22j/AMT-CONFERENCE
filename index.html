Why Entrepreneurship?

  • Economic Growth

    Entrepreneurs market economies and act as the wheels of the economic growth of the country.

  • Innovation

    Entrepreneurs bring new innovation that opens the door of new ventures, markets, products, and technology.

  • Social Changes

    Entrepreneurs change the cultures of society and reduce the dependency on obsolete methods, systems, and technologies.

  • National income

    New improved offerings, products or technologies from entrepreneurs enable new markets to be developed and new wealth created.

  • New Business

    The stimulation of related businesses or sectors that support the new venture add to further economic development.
